KKR's 2024 Trophy Journey - As a Kerala KKR Fan

As a Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR) fan since 2015, I have lived through thrilling wins and heartbreaking losses with no one beside me as in kerala ,KKR have very less fans and on my region there was me alone . But 2024 was special – we reclaimed the trophy ending a 10 year drought of IPL trophy with an incredible performance! From the very first match, the energy in the squad was electrifying which throughout made the team to fight the hardships.
So at the end it's not about popularity,number of fans or the count of Star players in your team.It's all about how you play the game .

Victory of Values

KKR’s 2024 title was not just about talent but the perfect mix of hard work, patience, teamwork, and perseverance. Every player stepped up in crucial moments, showing resilience even in tough matches. The team never gave up despite setbacks and fought till the end. It's not about a star player or a key performer ,KKR was a team of stars who aced their roles at perfect time.

Whether it was batsmen building partnerships, bowlers keeping calm under pressure, or fielders saving crucial runs, every member played a vital role. The never-give-up attitude truly defined KKR’s journey this season.

Gautam Gambhir's Impact

One of the biggest turning points for KKR in 2024 was the return of Gautam Gambhir as a mentor. How can I thank this man!Our very own GG,who was the special ingredient of this campaign. His leadership in the dugout revived KKR’s fearless approach, bringing back the same aggressive and strategic mindset that helped us lift the trophy in 2012 and 2014. His decisions of Narine's comeback as a opener,backing of Starc,Russel at death overs and utilization of young talents Angkrish and Ramandeep properly were top notch.

His influence on Shreyas Iyer’s captaincy, player strategies, and team mentality was evident throughout the season. Gambhir emphasized discipline, self-belief, and playing for the team, which created a winning mindset among the players.

Celebrating the Victory

After waiting 10 years, seeing KKR lift the trophy again was an emotional moment for me. It was personal for me as after years of dissapointments,shamefulness,hardships,We won. That also with a underdog lineup in the kkr fashion thrashing the mighty SRH in the final.
