Welcome to Wayanad

Wayanad is a beautiful district in Kerala, India, known for its lush greenery, wildlife, and serene landscapes.

About Wayanad

Wayanad is situated in the Western Ghats and is a popular destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

Tourist Attractions

  • Edakkal Caves
  • Chembra Peak
  • Banasura Sagar Dam
  • Soochipara Waterfalls
  • Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary

Culture and Heritage

Wayanad is home to indigenous tribal communities and has a rich cultural heritage. The district hosts various festivals and traditional art forms.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Wayanad is from October to May when the weather is pleasant for sightseeing and trekking.

How to Reach

By Air: The nearest airport is Calicut International Airport.

By Train: Kozhikode Railway Station is the nearest major railway station.

By Road: Wayanad is well-connected by roads from major cities in Kerala and Karnataka.
